Non Linear Model Predictive Control to aid Cooperative Localization

  1. Home
  2. Projects

Our team proposed an innovative nonlinear model predictive control (NMPC) scheme to address the challenges of localizing and planning the path of multiple unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs) in GPS-denied environments. Our algorithm computes optimal control inputs for the vehicles to cooperate and successfully transit from a source location to a destination, while ensuring that the chosen path covers enough landmarks for localization. To estimate vehicle positions, we utilized an Extended Kalman Filter (EKF) and only relative bearing measurements were used. Our approach demonstrates the potential of NMPC for efficient and effective multi-UAV path planning and localization in challenging environments.
